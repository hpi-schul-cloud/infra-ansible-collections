Example: Ensuring Integrity of Audit Logs with Digital Signatures
Scenario
Imagine you have a system that logs every administrative action taken on your server, such as creating new users, 
changing permissions, or modifying configurations. These logs are stored as audit logs.
To ensure these logs are not tampered with, PrivacyIDEA uses digital signatures.
